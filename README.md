# Tableau-Financial-Market-Investment
This project analyzes a large financial dataset containing 100,000+ records related to market performance, investment behavior, profitability, and risk assessment. The goal is to uncover trends in returns, risk levels, asset performance, and financial health indicators to support better investment and financial decision-making.

# Dataset Description
- The dataset consists of 17 financial and market-related variables, including:
- Volume – Trading volume of the asset
- Revenue – Total revenue generated
- Net Profit – Profit after expenses
- Operating Income – Income from core operations
- Debt-to-Equity Ratio – Measure of financial leverage
- Cash Flow – Net cash inflow/outflow
- Profit Margin – Profitability indicator
- Buy Orders & Sell Orders – Market demand and supply indicators
- Net Inflows – Net capital movement
- Volatility Index – Market risk indicator
- Investment Amount – Capital invested
- Current Value – Present value of the investment
- ROI Percentage – Return on investment
- Risk Category – Low / Medium / High risk classification
- Asset Type – Crypto, Stocks, Bonds, Real Estate, etc.
- Date – Transaction or record date

# Objectives
- Analyze investment performance using ROI and profitability metrics
- Identify high-risk vs low-risk investment behavior
- Study the impact of volatility on returns
- Compare asset types based on risk and returns
- Evaluate market demand trends using buy/sell orders
- Track financial performance over time

# Conclusion
This project demonstrates how financial and investment data can be transformed into actionable insights by analyzing risk, volatility, and return patterns. It highlights the importance of risk management, asset diversification, and performance tracking in financial decision-making.
